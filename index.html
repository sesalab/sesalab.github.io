About

The Software Engineering in Salerno Lab (SESA Lab) aims at bringing together the different research activities on software engineering carried out at the University of Salerno,to exploit synergies among the members and disseminate the most relevant research results.

The members of SESA Lab have competences in different fields of Software Engineering, including:

  • Empirical Software Engineering
  • Software Maintenance
  • Software Metrics
  • Software Processes
  • Software Quality
  • Software Reengineering
  • Software Testing
  • Source Code Analysis
  • Reverse Engineering
  • Cloud Computing
  • Code Smell Detection and Refactoring
  • Defect Prediction
  • Green Software Engineering
  • Mobile Computing
  • Search Based Software Engineering
  • Software Cost Estimation
  • Web Engineering
  • Configuration Management
  • Traceability Management
  • Software Visualization
  • Visual Languages
  • Document Management
  • Workflow Management
  • Business Processes
  • E-Learning

International Summer School on Software Engineering

The International Summer School on Software Engineering (ISSSE) is an annual event organised at the University of Salerno that intends to provide a contribution to Ph.D. students and academic and industrial researchers on latest findings in the field of Software Engineering.

The invited lecturers are internationally recognised research leaders covering different and very relevant topics for the scientific community.

The program of the school aims at creating discussion forums between lecturers and industrial and academic attendees.

Submission of student talks are also be encouraged to further stimulate the discussion.

More info at http://www.sesa.unisa.it/seschool/
